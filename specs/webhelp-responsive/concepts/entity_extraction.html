Entity modeling

The transformation from MARC to Linked Art requires that both records and access points within records be modeled and entified in order to create a normalized representation of the data.

  • Unique identifiers in the form of an IRI must be assigned to each entity.

  • Entities corresponding to access points (for example, 1XX, 6XX, and 7XX fields in MARC) must be extracted and merged (deduplicated).

  • Entities corresponding to records (bibliographic and holdings) should not be merged and deduplicated at this stage. For example, multiple editions of the novel Beloved will not be FRBRized or linked to a common Work entity. However, they should all be linked to a single Person entity for the author Toni Morrison.

  • A JSON-LD representation of each top-level entity must be dereferenceable through a RESTful API.
